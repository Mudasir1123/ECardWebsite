Frequently Asked Questions

You can change your subscription plan at any time by going to the Subscriptions page in your account settings. If you upgrade, the new plan will take effect immediately. If you downgrade, the new plan will take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

No, you won't lose your cards if you downgrade. However, if you have more cards than the new plan allows, you won't be able to create new cards until you're under the limit. You'll still be able to view and edit your existing cards.

Your billing cycle starts on the day you subscribe. For monthly plans, you'll be billed on the same day each month. For annual plans, you'll be billed once a year on the anniversary of your subscription date. You can view your next billing date in the Subscriptions page.

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. If you cancel, your subscription will remain active until the end of your current billing cycle. After that, your account will be downgraded to the Free plan. You won't receive a refund for the unused portion of your subscription.

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for new subscriptions. If you're not satisfied with your subscription within the first 14 days, contact our support team for a full refund. After 14 days, we do not offer refunds for canceled subscriptions or unused portions of your subscription.
